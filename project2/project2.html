muslims are required to pray 5 times a day. before sunrise, mid-day, afternoon, after sunset, and evening. muslims can pray anywhere that is quiet and clean but finding a place and time can be difficult. i have created a guide for muslims and others to spend time with the elements linked to prayer. imagery, different mosques in the country, tips on how to pray comfortably, how to pray, and where to pray on a college campus can be found on here. if you would like to submit an image or mosque please email me at @/sakina.pervez@gmail.com
